Salzburg Run Map

Servus! Welcome to my collection of favorite running routes in Salzburg! Because of uni and work in the afternoons, I usually end up going for runs after midnight (And now you know, why this map has a dark theme). But don’t worry! Salzburg is a peaceful city where not much happens at night. These routes are great for running both during the day and late at night!

This map was created by Maciej Grajzer as part of the course Geovisualization and Advanced Cartography at Paris Lodron Universität Salzburg, 2025
